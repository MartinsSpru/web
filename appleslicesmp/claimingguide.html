Claiming

1.

yYou must have a wall around your land except the water borders

2.

To claim anything you have to build a building (fully player built and consisting of atleast a 100 blocks 100 block tall towers dont count) and then you can claim anything in a radius of 50 blocks, any other builds count too

3.

If you want to claim an island then just build things atleast 50 blocks from the coast as stated in rule 2

If the whole coastline is in the radius of your buildings then you can claim the whole island

Country or Playerland?

when claiming you have to choose between making a country or a playerland

Country

  1. Work together with others
  2. Make markets and sell things
  3. start wars with other countries

Playerland

  1. Be alone by yourself
  2. Cant be attacked by other countries or Playerlands
  3. Cant start wars
  4. Everything is 100% yours and theres no risk of loss in wars since they cant start against you

What to do after your done with everything:

Message the server owner and ask for him to give you a claim on the map, when doing so please specify:

  1. Country or PLayerland
  2. The countries name if you chose country
  3. hex code of the color you want
  4. a pic of the map, please point out the area that your claim is at
