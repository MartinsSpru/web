Wars

Wars can only be fought between countries, playerlands cant participate in wars

to declare a war you have to go to #wars on the discord and @ the countrie you want to declare war on say a wargoal and that your declaring war

now your not at war yet, now you both have to decide on a time (14:00-19:00 Riga time)

wars last exactly 1 hour

if you lose then then your war goal doesnt get enforced onn the enemy

The first country to get 20 Kills Wins

if you lose then that country has to give you what you asked for in the war goal

during wars you can destroy, kill, rob the enemy

after a war you get a 10 day truce with that country

remember to carefully read the war rules

War Goals:

you can only chose one at a time

  1. Take land (cant be more than 20% of the enemies total land mass)
  2. Take Money
  3. puppet goverment (you can only chose this is your country is atleast 5 times bigger than the other country in land)
  4. independance war + take 20% of occopiers neighbouring land (land can only be taken if your neighbouring your overloard and this war goal can only be used if you are a puppet)

if your an indepenant country and have diplomatic ties/an alliance with other countries you can call them to help you in wars, they should if they can but if they dont your alliance breaks
