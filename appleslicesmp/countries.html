Country Features:

  1. Work together with others
  2. Make markets and sell things
  3. start wars with other countries

How to make a market

you need to build a main building for the market that is atleast 20 blocks big

the market zone must be a square or a rectangle you need to build a border around the zones border with signs, the furthest the signs can be from the main market building is 50 blocks

How to sell things in the market

place down a chest in the market zone

hold the item you want to sell in your hand

do /ezchestshop create (buy price) (sell price)

to edit your shops settings press on the shop if you press the redstone then you will be able to put things in your shop
