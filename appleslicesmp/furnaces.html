Upgradeable Furnaces

These are special furnaces that can only be bought with money and can be upgraded to be faster version of regular versions

How to get them

If you want the upgrade-able furnace you have do /furnace get

after placing it down you can pick it up with the menu in /furnace list, you can upgrade it there too, you can also change permisions for your furnaces there
