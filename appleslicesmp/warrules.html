War Rules

1.

To start a War your country must be 10 days old (irl)

2.

Playerlands cant participate in wars and cant be attacked

3.

Using and Creating warps during wars will get you banned for a day

4.

Withers are banned in wars

5.

Redstone machines of destruction and/or flying are banned during wars

6.

Elytras are Banned during wars

7.

You cant declare wars during a truce

8.

You cant use Explosives during wars
